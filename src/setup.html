Backbone

Sit with backbone 🦴

Backbone watches your posture through your webcam and nudges you when you slouch — plus eye breaks and stretch breaks to keep you moving.

Everything runs on your Mac. No video is recorded or uploaded — only on-device math. Let's get you set up in about a minute.

Turn on the camera

Backbone needs your webcam to see your posture. Click below — macOS will ask for camera permission the first time.

Camera off

Using an iPhone (Continuity Camera)? For the widest, steadiest view, turn off Center Stage in Control Center → Video Effects — it crops and pans, which throws off calibration.

Calibrate your “good” posture

A live camera window just opened. Get into your best posture — roll your shoulders back, lift your chin, sit tall — until the on-screen guide turns green, then press Calibrate right on that window.

Waiting for calibration…

This continues automatically once you've calibrated. You can re-calibrate anytime from the menu bar.

Break reminders

Sensible defaults below — tweak now or later in Settings.

Buzz my phone & watch (optional)

Get a haptic tap on your smart watch (Apple Watch, Garmin, Galaxy Watch…) when you slouch — using the free ntfy app. Skip this if you don't want it.

Setup:

1. Install ntfy on your iPhone — get it on the App Store ↗, or scan this code with your phone camera.
  1. Open ntfy → tap “+” → Subscribe to topic → type your exact topic above.
  2. Come back and hit Send test buzz — you should feel it.

How Backbone works

🦴
Menu bar — the spine icon shows 🟢 good or 🟡 slouching. Click it to pause, calibrate, take a break, or open stats.
👀
Gentle nudges — slouch for a few seconds and you'll get an on-screen cue telling you exactly what to fix, plus an optional watch buzz.
🧘
Breaks — quick eye breaks and longer stretch breaks appear on your schedule. Skip or postpone anytime.
📈
Stats — your streak, good-posture %, and trends live in the Stats dashboard.
Get the most out of it
↻
Re-calibrate whenever you change your chair, desk height, or camera.
🎚️
Too touchy or not enough? Tune Sensitivity in Settings — changes apply live.
📷
On an iPhone (Continuity Camera), keep Center Stage off for steady tracking.

You're all set 🎉

Backbone now lives in your menu bar — click the spine icon anytime to pause, take a break, see stats, or open settings. Settings will open next so you can fine-tune everything.

Tip: ⌥⌘B starts a stretch break · ⌥⌘P pauses monitoring · ⌥⌘S snoozes breaks.
